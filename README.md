Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a routine?
Tap "Create Routine" on the home screen. Add tasks, set time allocations for each, and choose which days the routine runs.

What is the Ghost Race?
When no family members are racing, you can race against your personal "ghost" — a virtual opponent that moves at your historical average pace. It keeps routines motivating even when you're solo.

How do streaks work?
Complete your routine on time each day to build a streak. Your current and longest streaks are displayed on the home screen.

Can I use the app with my family?
Yes! Create profiles for each family member. When multiple profiles run routines at the same time, a Family Race appears showing everyone's progress.

Privacy

ADHDO List stores all data locally on your device using iCloud sync for family features. We do not collect personal data or share information with third parties.
